Chicken Namban

If you enjoy succulent chicken dishes with creamy sauces, this chicken nanban recipe is for you. Chicken nanban is an example of “yoshoku”, Western style food that has been adapted to suit Japanese tastes. This succulent chicken dish originated in Miyazaki Prefecture in the far South of Japan. Topped off with rich tartar sauce, this dish is particularly nice with a side of chips or new potatoes.
